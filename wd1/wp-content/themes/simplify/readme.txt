Simplify Professional Responsive Theme

== Description ==

Simplify was listed among the top 5 most popular Themes many days. Simplify is a CSS3 Powered and WordPress Latest Version Ready Responsive Theme for Small Business Companies to any purposes. Exciting Slide, 02 Rows of Featured Boxes, Customizable Background and other options will give the WordPress Driven Site an attractive look.  Simplify is super Elegant and Professional Responsive Theme which will be adjusted automatically with all smart devices. First and Second Row Featured Boxes can show your company's Featured Items, Services etc.  Right and Footer Sidebar will be usable for showing the widgets. Full Wide Template can be used for showing showcase or other pages. Simplify is Translation Ready.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What to do after Theme Installation ? =
After installing the Theme Please go to Appearance > Customize. You can set the Slide Images, Featured Boxes, Featured Contents, Social Links and other Theme Options/Settings there. You can set the Logo from Appearance > Header.  


= Why is Simplify? =
Simplify is for Personal and Corporate Web Sites

= Can it be used for Personal or Company Websites? =
You can use for Personal to Corporate Sites. It is better for the Small Business Companies

= Is it only for Blogs ? =
No, It is for Blogs, Company, Personal Websites etc.
